Everyone’s present and future is dependent on Artificial Intelligence (AI). Being “AI literate” should not be a privilege. Our mission is to make AI an accessible skill to all, and offer a hands-on experience along with a comprehensive approach to a broad range of AI concepts.
Hi-AI aims to promote online AI education and empower everyone from age 9 to 109 with freely accessible AI knowledge. By offering human-centered, inclusive, appealing, and rigorous AI educational resources, we hope to break barriers of age, gender, language, and wealth in AI education.
