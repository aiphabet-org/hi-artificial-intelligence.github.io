Welcome to Hi-AI, a free online curriculum for learning Artificial Intelligence. There are no prerequisites, and no prior knowledge is required. The curriculum is comprehensive and rigorous, yet we designed it to be accessible and inclusive.

We also recommend you to check the Background section before you start the modules to equip yourself with the necessary skills for learning AI. You can navigate through the modules and all other content through the navigation bar on the left side of the screen.
